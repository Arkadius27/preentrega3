KNOW OUR SOLUTIONS

IaaS, Infrastructure as a Service


It is one of the three fundamental models in the field of cloud computing, together with the Platform as a Service (PaaS, Platform as a Service) and the Software as a Service (SaaS, Software as a Service). Like all cloud services, the IaaS offered by Servidores del Sur provides access to computing resources located in a virtualized environment, the “cloud”.

SaaS, Software as a Service


The concept of Software as a Service (SaaS, Software as a Service) describes any cloud service in which consumers can access software applications over the internet. These applications are hosted “in the cloud” and can be used for a wide variety of tasks, both for individuals and organizations. Business users can use applications to solve a wide range of needs, from accounting and billing to sales tracking, planning, performance monitoring and communications (for example, webmail and instant messaging).

PaaS, Plataform as a Service


The concept of Platform as a Service is a category of cloud services that provides a platform and an environment that allow developers to create applications and services that work through the internet. PaaS services are hosted in the cloud, and users can simply access them through their web browser or remote desktop.

PaaS services consist of preconfigured functionalities that customers can subscribe to, choosing the functions they want to include to solve their needs and discarding those they do not need.
